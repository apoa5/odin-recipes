Pancake

This is a pancake recipe that my family loves, and it is very easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2-and-a-half tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons salt
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Whisk all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, eggs, milk, salt and vanilla extract together in a bowl till you get the consistensy you want.
  2. Buter a non-stick pan on medium heat.
  3. Add in the pancake batter in pancake sized portions and cook until golden brown on both sides.
